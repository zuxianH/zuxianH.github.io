Who am I ?

“knowing yourself is the beginning of all wisdom.” -Aristotle

“knowing other is wisdom knowing the self is enlightenment” -Lao Tzu

More than two thousand years ago, some people already began to think about self-consciousness. Aristoteles(384–322 BC) and Lao Tzu (600 BC) were the most famous ideologists and philosophers at the same period. The thing which surprised me is Lao Tzu came from Ancient China, meanwhile Aristoteles was från Ancient Greece and they both realized the importance of self-identity. Since these two independently developed civilizations can have such the same ideas. It really surprised me. This kind of wisdom may not be truly understood by many people in today’s society. What I believe people nowadays pursue more material life than spirit of life.

Where I want to go?

“I come from the great Tang dynasty of the east land and I’m going to pilgrim the budda for lection.” -Tang Sanzang

This quote is coming from the chinese novel “Journey to the West”, it is one of the four great classical novels of chinese literature. This novel was published in the 16th century during the Ming dynasty and talked about the story of Tang Sanzang and his apprentices Sun Wukong, Zhu Bajie and Sha Wujing and going to the west to learn the scripture. Later it adapted from a novel into a TV series. As a child this was one of my favourite TV serier. I am atheist but this story really impacted me a lot.

This sentence is difficult to express its essence in other languages than chinese. It it little werid when I read it in english.
