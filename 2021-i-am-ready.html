2021, I am ready

This article was last updated: 5 days ago

2020 is an extraordinary year. During this year, I have experienced a lot, grew a lot, and learned a lot. In the face of the sudden outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic, it changed everything suddenly. During this period we were all forced to take “cloud lessons” and I was also forced to take classes at home. At the beginning, there were a lot of things that I was not used to, but it gradually became numb, and did not care that much. At the same time during the pandemic I also learned a new instrument, the violin. Another important event that changed the direction of my life was that in July I got the offer from the Uppsala University. This also mean that I will start a new life in Uppsala. In September, I came to this strange city alone. A city with no friends, no relatives, and no reliance is here to start my new life. I learned that how to reads alone, study alone, think alone, cook alone, and live alone. Perhaps that is what growth up mean. Time rushed into December, and a short semester passed. I still do not regret my decision. In life, I think is should go around and see more, learn more about the new environment, and open horizons about the world.

On New Year’s Eve, I am alone in the dark and empty room to write this blog. I am not a traditional person or person who has a sense of ritual. Although 2020 is indeed extraordinary, I hope that this new year 2021 will also have new hope, new motivation, and new meaning. Time flies really fast, and my 21 years have passed like in a moment. We all say goodbye to the people and things in the past at that crossroad. While I looking back now, it only myself is around. The journey of life is so far, I hope to meet a better myself in the front.

I have set a few goals in this new year.

  • Start the four theoretical mechanics and understand them
    • Quantum mechanics
    • Electrodynamics
    • Theoretical Mechanics
    • Statistical Mechanics
  • Participate the college physics competitions
  • As in previous years, I still need to improve my English
  • the world is so big I want to see and travel around especially China
  • Better biological clock, better time management, better character
  • Stay away from social media
  • I want to read more books this year and learn more.
  • Improved my violin skills

Don’t forget to love yourself in this strange city.

2020是一个不平凡的一年，在这一年经历了很多，成长了很多，见识了很多。面对突如其来的新冠肺炎，我们都防不胜防。在这期间我们都被迫上“云课”，我也被迫在家上课。刚开始有很多东西不习惯，但慢慢的也就麻木了，无所谓了。同时我疫情期间也学习了一件新的乐器，小提琴。另外一个改变我人生方向的重大事件是在七月份我拿到了乌普萨拉物理系的录取通知书。这也将意味着我要在乌普萨拉开启新的生活。今年的九月我孤身一人来到这陌生的城市。无朋友，无亲戚，无依靠的城市这来开启我的新生活。我学会了一个人看书，一个人学习，一个人思考，一个人做饭，一个人生活。或许那就是成长把。时间匆匆忙忙到了十二月，短短的一个大学学期就过去了。我仍然没有后悔当初的决定。人生把，我觉得就应该多去看看，多去了解一下新的环境，开开自己的眼界。

新年前夕我一个人在乎黑忽暗,空虚的房间写这篇文章。我并不是一个传统的，有仪式感的人。虽然2020的确不平凡但我希望这这新的一年也有新的希望，新动力，以及新的意义。时间过得真的很快，匆匆忙忙我的21年如同一刹那间度过。曾经的人，曾经的事我们都在那十字路口里告别。如今回过头来,周围只剩我自己。人生路程如此遥远，希望在前面遇见更好的自己。

在这新的一年里我立下几个目标。

  • 开始四大力学，以及了解他们
    • 量子力学
    • 电动力学
    • 理论力学
    • 统计力学
  • 参加大学物理比赛
  • 像往年一样，我英语还是需要有待提高
  • 我想回中国看看，世界那么大我想去看看
  • 更好的生物钟，更好的时间管理，更好的人品
  • 远离社交媒体
  • 有时候我发现我读的书很少，我想在这一年内读多些书。
  • 提高的我小提琴的技巧

在这陌生的城市别忘了爱自己。
